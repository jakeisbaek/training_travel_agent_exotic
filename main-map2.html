What are the TOP 8 exclusive destinations in Caribbean?
You can check the destinations and their locations below.

.Turks & Caicos .Saint Kitts & Navis .Grenada .Saint Lucia .Saint Maarten .Barbados .Antigua .Grand Cayman Islands

Turks & Caicos

  • British oversea territory
  • Located in Eastern Caribbean
  • famous for the softest white sand beaches and one of the the largest reef systems

Saint Kitts & Navis

  • Located in Eastern Caribbean
  • Formed by volcanoes (which are dormant now)
  • famous for tropical forest activities & cultural events

Grenada

  • Located in Southern Caribbean
  • The spice of Caribbean
  • famous for its own culinary with rum distilleraries and chocolate factories

Saint Lucia

  • Located in Southern Caribbean
  • Well known for the beautiful landscape
  • famous for natural hot pools and bubbling mud springs

Saint Maarten

  • Located in Eastern Caribbean
  • Divided between two countries (Dutch and French side)
  • famous for Maho beach where you can see planes landing just feet above the beach

Barbados

  • Located in Southern Caribbean
  • Provides lots of activities in the ocean and forest
  • famous for rich culture with music & drinks

Antigua

  • Located in Eastern Caribbean
  • The sunniest of the Eastern Caribbean Islands with low humidity
  • Lot of water activities

Grand Cayman Islands

  • Located in Western Caribbean
  • British oversea territory
  • famous for seven mile beach wich is one of the best beaches in the world
